Welcome to KnitsbyKit!

my name is Kitty an am currently on my first year of uni. this page is where I will be sharing my BA textiles projects and personal pieces

I started off with hand knitting on my gap year when I was ready to prepare myself for a BA sociology degree. however, starting my hand knitting journey switched my route entirely! Starting off with a foundation course in 2022, I am now currently studying a BA textiles degree. This page is where I will be sharing my woven pieces, machine knits and my hand knitted pieces. On my textile journey, I have fallen in love with process of the knit machines where I have so far learnt new techniques such as pointelle, ladders and stripes. Also, I have been lucky to learn to warp my own loom which was challenging, but has encouraged me to push myself.
